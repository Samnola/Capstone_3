
## Overview

For this capstone project, I analyzed four years of sales data for EmporiUm using Power BI. The analysis focused on the Northeast region and was designed to identify sales trends, top-performing states, bestselling books, and online sales performance.

## Report Pages

### Northeast Sales Overview
- Total Revenue
- Average Monthly Revenue
- Top Performing State
- Sales Trends
- Sales by Category
- Sales by State

### Best Sellers
- Total Book Revenue
- Top Selling Author
- Best-Selling Books
- Book Sales by State

### Online Sales
- Total Online Sales
- Highest Revenue State
- Online Sales Map
- Top 5 States by Online Sales

## Bonus Features
- Mobile Report Layout
- Interactive Slicers and Filters
- Additional Online Sales Analysis Page
